Zhongping Zhang

I am a first year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Boston University. My advisor is Prof. Bryan Plummer. I received my bachelor's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2016, and my master degree from University of Rochester in 2018. My research interest spans Computer Vision and its relations to Data Mining, Image Processing, Natural Language Processing, etc. My CV can be found here.
